Rules

Read all about the general rules of dart

  • The dart board has to be at exactly 173cm above the ground at its center.
  • Each player gets 3 throws, after which it's the next players' turn.
  • Optional: The counting of each players' throws begins once they have hit a Bullseye, a double or a triple.
  • You have to get to exactly 0 points in one round or you wont win.
